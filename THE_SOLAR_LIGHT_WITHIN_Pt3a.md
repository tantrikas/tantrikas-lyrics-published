# THE SOLAR LIGHT WITHIN - Pt.3a

The conscious and subconscious move together as one  
And so do the nerve paths under the sun  
The cerebro-spinal serves the waking mind  
Receiving the senses, directing the spine  

Its center is seated up in the brain  
Where reason examines each thought that we frame  
The sympathetic works silent below  
Where hidden life currents unceasingly flow  

Its center is held in the solar fire  
Behind the stomach, source of inner desire  
The Solar Plexus, radiant and deep  
Guides all the vital functions we keep  

The vagus nerve bridges the upper and lower  
Linking the systems with living power  
From the brain to the chest it travels on  
Touching heart and lungs as it passes along  

Then through the diaphragm, shedding its sheath  
It joins the deep currents underneath  
So man is made one in body and flame  
A single whole, not divided in name  

Each thought is first taken up in the brain  
Tested by reason again and again  
And once believed true by the conscious sight  
It is sent below to be formed in life  

Sent to the Solar Plexus, the subjective brain  
To be woven in flesh, to appear in the plain  
Brought into the world as visible fact  
Beyond debate, beyond counteract  

The subconscious never argues a case  
It accepts the conclusion and moves into place  
It acts on the verdict the conscious mind gave  
And builds it in silence, faithful and brave  

The Solar Plexus is the body’s sun  
A center of power where energies run  
Distributing force through nerve and vein  
Sending life outward again and again  

This energy is real, this sun is real  
Its currents can nourish, restore, and heal  
It moves through the body to every part  
And radiates outward from mind and heart  

An atmosphere forms around our frame  
A living field, a magnetic flame  
If this radiation is steady and strong  
We call that person magnetic among  

Filled with a presence that comforts the weak  
Bringing peace before they even speak  
Their nearness alone may quiet the mind  
And leave troubled spirits more balanced, more kind  

When the Solar Plexus is active and bright  
It pours vitality, warmth, and light  
To every organ, to every nerve  
Giving the body the strength to serve  

And all who meet such a soul may feel  
A pleasant current, gentle and real  
But when that radiation is checked or confined  
Unpleasant sensations trouble the mind  

The flow of life to a part is stopped  
And many conditions are thereby dropped  
Physical illness may rise from there  
When force no longer nourishes with care  

Mental weakness may also appear  
When conscious thought loses its vital sphere  
For mind depends on the deeper source  
To sustain its action with living force  

And outer conditions can darken too  
When the Universal link is broken through  
For the Solar Plexus is where part meets Whole  
Where finite touches the Infinite Soul  

Where the uncreate enters the realm of form  
Where the unseen becomes living and warm  
Where life appears from the hidden sea  
And flows without limit through you and me
