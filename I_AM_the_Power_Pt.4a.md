# I AM the Power-Pt.4a

The “I” is not the body you see  
Just an instrument it’s meant to be  
The “I” is not the mind that thinks  
Another tool beneath its links  

The “I” commands, directs the flow  
Tells mind and body where to go  
When you realize what “I” is  
You’ll feel a power unlike this  

Your traits and habits, all you claim  
Are shaped by thought, not who you name  
Personality fades away  
The real “I” was never clay  

“I think,” the mind obeys the call  
“I go,” the body moves in all  
The “I” is spirit, deep inside  
The source of power you can’t deny  

The greatest gift this “I” can wield  
Is thought, where all creation’s sealed  
Yet few can think in truth or light  
So results stay weak, confined, and slight  

Selfish thoughts will plant defeat  
An infant mind repeats, repeats  
A mature mind learns the truth  
All must gain from every move  

No gain through weakness, none through need  
Such acts will always plant bad seed  
For you are part of something whole  
The Universal, living soul  

No part can war against the rest  
All thrive when all are truly blessed  
Those who know this live with ease  
They calm the mind, their thoughts they seize  

No wasted time, no scattered aim  
No chasing things that bring no gain  
They focus high, they concentrate  
And shape their lives, create their fate  

If you can’t yet, then now begin  
The work must start, the change within  
Effort given returns in kind  
Exact proportion, law defined  

“I can be what I will to be”  
Let that truth set your spirit free  
Repeat it till you understand  
The “I” that holds creation’s hand  

Know this “I” and you’ll become  
Invincible beneath the sun  
If purpose pure and thoughts align  
With universal, grand design  

Repeat it morning, night, all day  
Until it lives in you always  
Form the habit, make it stay  
Let no distraction lead astray  

For unfinished paths create  
The habit pattern known as fate  
If you begin, then see it through  
Let nothing stand between you  

Decide once, and it is done  
No more debate, no need to run  
The die is cast, the will is strong  
The “I” has spoken all along
