# THE SOLAR LIGHT WITHIN — Pt. 3b

This center of energy, mighty and wise  
Touches all life beneath all skies  
Omnipotent in its inward span  
Because it is joined to the Source of man  

To all intelligence, all living flame  
And it can accomplish what thought may name  

For here lies the power the conscious can hold  
To plant an idea in the silent gold  

The subconscious will carry the pattern through  
In forms and events made living and true  
So conscious thought is master here  
Of this sun center so bright and clear  

And the quality of thought that we keep  
Is the quality sent to the levels deep  
The kind of thought we steadily raise  
Will tint the radiance of all our days  

The character, nature, and inward tone  
Become the harvest that we have sown  
So let your light shine, let it expand  
Let energy move through heart and hand  

The more we radiate living force  
The faster we alter the outer course  
Turning hard conditions by inner light  
Into joy and profit, clear and bright  

Then comes the question: how shall it shine?  
How shall we strengthen this sun divine?  
Non-resistant thought makes the center expand  
Resistant thought contracts its command  

Pleasant thought opens the inward gate  
Unpleasant thought narrows the state  
Thoughts of courage, of hope, of power  
Strengthen the soul from hour to hour  

Confidence widens the field within  
And calls fresh life to rise again  
But fear is the enemy, dark and grim  
The cloud that gathers and covers the rim  

Fear hides the sun in a lifelong gray  
And steals the soul’s clear strength away  
It makes men fear the days gone by  
Fear the present and question why  

Fear the future, fear their own name  
Fear both friend and foe the same  
Fear everything and everyone near  
Until all life bends low to fear  

But when fear is banished, uprooted, gone  
The clouds will part, the light comes on  
Then power and life are found at last  
And the soul comes free from the prisoned past  

When you know you are one with Infinite Power  
And prove it in some transforming hour  
By overcoming adverse conditions through thought  
You will fear nothing that life has brought  

Then fear is destroyed, your birthright restored  
And strength is felt, not merely adored  

Our attitude toward life decides  

What kind of experience with us abides  
If we expect nothing, little appears  
If we demand greatly, abundance draws near  
The world seems harsh when we fail to stand  

And make room boldly for what we have planned  
Criticism crushes the timid heart  
And keeps many noble ideas apart  
But one who knows the Solar Plexus flame  

Will not bow down to blame or shame  
He radiates courage, confidence, might  
Anticipates success by inward sight  
Breaks through barriers, crosses doubt  

Leaps hesitation and casts fear out  
Practice this truth: be still, relax  
Let muscles loosen, release the tax  
Then peace, free blood, and freedom will flow  
And the Solar Plexus will brightly glow
